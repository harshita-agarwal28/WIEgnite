Why Cleanups Matter

Cleanups not only improve the appearance of our communities but also have a positive impact on the environment and public health. By removing litter and waste, we can prevent pollution and protect wildlife.

How to Participate

Joining a local cleanup is easy! Simply find an upcoming event in your area, gather some friends or family, and head to the designated cleanup site. Don't forget to wear comfortable clothes and bring gloves and trash bags.
